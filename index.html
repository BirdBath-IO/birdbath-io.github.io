What is BirdBath?

BirdBath is a tweet management and approval tool.

How can it help?

  • Let other people tweet from your account - without your password
  • Approve, edit or reject tweets before they are posted
  • Not just tweets - you can retweet too

Get BirdBath

BirdBath is open source - if you want to, you can host it yourself and it'll cost you nothing.
